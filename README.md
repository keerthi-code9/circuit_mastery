
> *Learning circuits by building, simulating, measuring, and reasoning.*

## 📖 About

This repository documents my journey of mastering electrical circuit design from first principles.

Instead of memorizing formulas, I study each concept by:
- Building practical circuits
- Running simulations
- Predicting outcomes before testing
- Comparing theory with experimental results
- Analyzing why the circuit behaves the way it does

The objective is to develop strong circuit intuition that forms the foundation for Analog Electronics, Power Electronics, Embedded Systems, and Hardware Design.

---

## 🎯 Goals

- Build intuition rather than memorize equations
- Verify every major circuit concept through simulation
- Understand the physical meaning behind electrical laws
- Develop practical debugging skills
- Maintain a public engineering lab notebook

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- LTspice / Circuit Simulator
- Multisim / Falstad / Tinkercad (where applicable)
- Breadboard (for hardware verification)
- Digital Multimeter (future experiments)
- Git & GitHub
